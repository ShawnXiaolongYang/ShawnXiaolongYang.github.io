When will people use Divvy Bike

Different type of users may use the divvy bike at different time period.
From left graph, we can find customers usage peaks at 15 o' clock, while subscribers usage peaks at both 7 and 17 o'clock.
It make sense since customers are mostly tourists, they would take a bike ride in the afternoon as a tour;
subscirbers are mostly local commutors, they would use the bike for commute in the morning and after work.
From the right graph, we can find customers use the bike mostly on the weekdends, while subscribers use the bike mostly on the weekdays.
Both of the graph match that customers are tourists and subscirbers are local commutors.
